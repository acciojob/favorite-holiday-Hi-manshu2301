Maldives


maldives is an island country nearby india and is preferred by many indian for their vaccation.

Top 5 attractions of maldives

  1. Ubud Monkey Forest – Explore the forest sanctuary with playful monkeys.
  2. Tanah Lot Temple – Famous sea temple for breathtaking sunsets.
  3. Mount Batur – Enjoy a sunrise hike up an active volcano.
  4. Uluwatu Temple – Perched on a cliff with stunning ocean views.
  5. Seminyak Beach – Perfect spot for surfing and sunset cocktails.

Must try foods of maldives

  • Babi Guling – Traditional Balinese roasted pork.
  • Nasi Campur – Mixed rice with a variety of side dishes.
  • Mie Goreng – Stir-fried noodles with vegetables and meat.
  • Sate Lilit – Minced seafood satay wrapped around lemongrass sticks.
  • Bubur Injin – Black rice pudding dessertdessert, sweet and creamy.
